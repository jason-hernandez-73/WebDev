Latitude vs. Cloudiness

        Cloud cover is highly variable at all latitudes, and we did not find any clear patterns. The horizontal clusters at several degrees of cloudiness might be an artifact of the way cloud cover is measured.

        Separating the hemispheres did not reveal any hidden patterns.
