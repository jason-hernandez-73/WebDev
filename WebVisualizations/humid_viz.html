Latitude vs. Humidity

        Humidity is highly variable at all latitudes, but there is one striking pattern: the lowest humidities were clustered around 20 degrees north. This is the "horse latitudes," where the Sahara desert and Arabian Peninsula are located. People who complain of humidity and think that "dry heat" is better would do well to ponder the implications.

        Separating the hemispheres showed that in both, the latitudes nearest the equator were the most humid. The Southern Hemisphere's driest points were spread over a larger latitudinal range than the Northern.
