Latitude vs. Wind

        Wind is highly variable at all latitudes, and we did not find any clear patterns, except that the highest outliers were at high latitudes.

        Separating the hemispheres did not reveal any hidden patterns. The Southern Hemisphere shows fewer data points overall, reflecting its higher proportion of ocean to land relative to the Northern Hemisphere. This might obscure its wind patterns somewhat: the notorious "Roaring Forties," "Furious Fifties," and "Screaming Sixties" occur in the Southern Ocean, where there are no cities.
